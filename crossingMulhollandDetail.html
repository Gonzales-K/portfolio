The Homepage

The navigation bar and footer will be constant throughout the site. The navigation bar will contain the logo as well as links to product collections, major business categories (contact info, etc.), the shopping cart, and socials. The footer will have contact info and any regulatory information.

The body of the page will have a hero section with the name of the business against a background image. Below that there will be a section with featured products which will have product images and names. The image will change on hover and if clicked will route to the product details.

Product Detail

The product detail page has 4 main sections. The description, product images, the product details, and additional information.
The description section is the sales pitch. The product images will be on a carousel which the user will be able to flip through. The product details will display the price, dimensions, estimated ship time, and have a button to add the item to their cart. Lastly, the additional information section will contain care instructions, the return policy, and FAQs.

The Cart

The cart will have three main sections. A table listing the items to be purchased and the cost of each, a section for the applicable charges and a button to checkout, and a section for additional items they may be interested in.
