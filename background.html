About Me

I'm a software engineer who is deeply passionate about the transformative power of programming to improve everyday life. Whether it's simplifying workflows with innovative features or creating entirely new applications, I firmly believe in technology's ability to make a positive impact.

My journey in software engineering has been marked by a continuous pursuit of knowledge and a commitment to delivering high-quality solutions. My experience spans across diverse industries, including the medical and logistics sectors. The thing that has stood out to me in every workplace is how much a single feature can impact the day to day lives of end-users and how that can also have a massive impact on the business. In fact that is what gave me the courage to make this career change.

As an ECG technician I experienced how the ability to zoom and invert the colors of the ecg tracing not only reduced eye-strain but improved productivity. Simply because it was easier to see. In my logistics role, there was a process that took many hours out of the day and was very prone to human error. I learned visual basic and built a tool that allowed me to complete my work in less than half a day, take on more projects, and assist colleagues with their workload all with very little error.

Building that tool and seeing the impact was the most fulfilling experience of my professional life. It made me realize the possibilities... and that I could contribute to those solutions. I get that feeling every time I come up against a programming challenge and solve it. That's what makes me determined to make this work my future.
